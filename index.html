How to Play

Each round plays a short fragment (max 5 seconds) of a real piano recording. As soon as it starts, three possible answers appear.

  1. Press Play Fragment to hear the clip.
  2. Pick the composer & work — the sooner you're right, the more it's worth. The metronome case fills up as your time runs out.
  3. Not sure, or want to move on? Hit Skip — it counts as wrong and advances.
  4. The game runs for 5 rounds. Your final score depends on both accuracy and speed.
