Program Note

How a round is played

No accounts, no tutorial video — the whole game is four beats long.

I.

Press play

A five-second fragment, starting somewhere unpredictable — never just the famous opening bars.

II.

Three choices appear

The instant the audio starts, so you can answer mid-fragment if you already know it.

III.

The case fills up

Twelve seconds to answer. Faster, correct answers are worth more — the metronome shows exactly how much time is left.

IV.

Five rounds, one tier

Your final score — accuracy and speed combined — earns you a rank from Newbie to Wizard.
